Welcome

This site aims to be a comprehensive guide to Workbench. We’ll cover topics such as getting Workbench up and running, creating and managing your data, customizing the way Workbench works and looks, deploying to various environments, and give you some advice on participating in the future development of Workbench itself.

So what is Workbench, exactly?

Workbench is a simple, fast, visualization tool for analyzing and visualizing your data. It takes a directory containing raw data files in various formats, runs it through converters, and spits out complete, web-based analysis and visualization tools.
